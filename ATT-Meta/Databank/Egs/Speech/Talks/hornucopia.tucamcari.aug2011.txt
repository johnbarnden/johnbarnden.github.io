At "Hornucopia" event, Tucamcari, New Mexico, USA, August 2011
==============================================================

{Heard by John Barnden. The event was a musical performance consisting
of people operating their car hooters.}

Announcer said:

  The white people don't want to take part ...

meaning the people with the white cars present.

He followed the comment with an embarrassed explanation of what he
meant.
