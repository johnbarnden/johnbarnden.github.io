From "Fifth Moon is Cold Comfort for Pluto"
===========================================
in New Scientist, 21 July 2012, p.11.

[NP]

The discovery of [new Pluto moon] P5 provides ammunition for those still
upset at Pluto's demotion from the ranks of fully fledged planets. "If
you're important enough to have acquired five satellites, you're a
planet," insists Kevin Baines, a planetary scientist at NASA's Jet
Propulsion Laboratory in Pasadena, California, who was not involved in
the discovery.
