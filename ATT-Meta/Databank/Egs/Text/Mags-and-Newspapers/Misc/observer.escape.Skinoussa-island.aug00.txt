From "Escape" section of The Observer newpaper, 13 August 2000
--------------------------------------------------------------

p.3:

{Re the Greek island of Skinoussa}

[NP] I met a sound-recordist from an award-winning film documentary
about Skinoussa. He is back on the island for the first time in around
20 years and trying to absorb the changes in a place candied in his
mind. He assures me his experience was the real thing. [EP]
