DESCRIPTION OF METAPHOR:

Emotional Mind as Physical Space

This category has been abandoned, as of 19 March 2020. It has been absorbed into MIND AS PHYSICAL SPACE.

The category was like Mind as Physical Space, except that it involved emotional rather than mental entities/events.

But I have never regarded emotional entities/events as strictly distinguishable from mental ones.
Therefore, arbitrary categorization decisions were sometimes being made about examples in the databank.
