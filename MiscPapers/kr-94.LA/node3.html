SKETCH OF REASONING

Here we informally and partially outline the main reasoning steps ATT-Meta takes for examples (1--1a), (1--1b) and (1--1c), conveying the rough flavor of its SR and metaphor-based reasoning and of their intimate interaction. We will touch on ATT-Meta's unusual feature of distinguishing conscious belief as an important special case of belief. The section also illustrates the uncertainty and defeasibility of ATT-Meta's reasoning, largely apparent below through the use of the qualifier ``presumably.''
